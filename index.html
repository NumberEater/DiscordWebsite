Gaming Discord Server

Donations

We do not require a donation to join and use our discord server but you do have an option to donate $2+ for a special rank on the server called Donator. The donator rank is able to:

  • Play music in any valid music channel
  • Gain access to a private donator lounge for gaming/music
  • Change your nickname

Staff Applications

If you would like to apply for staff, please fill out the google form listed below and when you are done, in the staff-applications text channel, write, "I have filled out a staff application." Once we recieve this message we will review your application and you will have an answer within 48 hours. To give you your answer, we will either send you an email or we will direct message you on Discord.
